About Us

We are the Academy of Medical Science Network (AMS) Partners in Tanzania & the UK, and the International Biometric Society (IBS) Tanzania Region. Our vision is to facilitate linkage, management, and analysis of routinely collected patient data. The purpose is to generate high-quality evidence to identify high-risk populations and recommend potential tailored public health interventions, particularly for people living in underserved communities in Tanzania.

We are currently funded by the AMS Networking Grant for a project titled "An international and interdisciplinary network for analysing routine HIV/AIDS and cancer registry data in Tanzania" . Thanks to this funding, we are able to organise two Networking Workshops in 2025 following the first IBS Tanzania Region R workshop.

Network Partners Workshops 2 & 3

We successfully ran Workshop 2 from 22 September to 3 October 2025, focusing on building sustainable partnerships to strengthen HIV and cancer data linkages by integrating hospital Care & Treatment Centre and population-based cancer registry (PBCR) data at Bugando Medical Centre, Tanzania.

Join us for Workshop 3 running from 10-14 November 2025 in Adden Palace Hotel, Mwanza, Tanzania, featuring exclusive lectures, interactive discussions and impactful networking opportunities with Biostatistical methodologists in HIV/AIDS and cancer from Tanzania, Kenya, South Africa and the UK.
